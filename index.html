Luciano Barcelos Porto

Musician, engineer and music producer.

With 3 albums recorded (2015, 2018, 2021), 3 international tours (2015, 2016, 2017) and 8 self produced singles, Luciano is a well known local musician.
From Belo Horizonte, the many bands he've played during the years has some interesting rock soundings.
